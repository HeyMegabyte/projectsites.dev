Project Sites

Content Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Project Sites by Megabyte LLC is an AI-powered website generation and hosting platform. This Content Policy outlines the guidelines for acceptable content created, hosted, and published through our Service. All users must comply with this policy.

Acceptable Use

Project Sites is designed to help small businesses and individuals create professional websites. Content hosted on our platform must be:

  • Lawful and compliant with all applicable local, state, federal, and international laws
  • Accurate and not intentionally misleading
  • Respectful and non-discriminatory
  • Original or properly licensed for use

Prohibited Content

The following types of content are strictly prohibited on Project Sites:

1. Illegal Content

  • Content that violates any applicable law or regulation
  • Content promoting or facilitating illegal activities
  • Content related to illegal drugs, controlled substances, or drug paraphernalia
  • Content involving the sale of counterfeit goods

2. Harmful or Dangerous Content

  • Content that promotes violence, self-harm, or harm to others
  • Content containing or distributing malware, viruses, or other harmful code
  • Content designed to phish, scam, or deceive users
  • Content promoting dangerous activities without appropriate warnings

3. Hateful or Discriminatory Content

  • Content that promotes hatred or discrimination based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or age
  • Content that harasses, bullies, or intimidates individuals or groups
  • Content that promotes extremist ideologies

4. Intellectual Property Violations

  • Content that infringes on copyrights, trademarks, patents, or other intellectual property rights
  • Unauthorized use of another person's or company's branding or identity
  • Content that misrepresents the origin or ownership of materials

5. Adult and Explicit Content

  • Pornographic or sexually explicit material
  • Content depicting or promoting the sexual exploitation of minors
  • Excessively graphic or violent imagery

6. Spam and Deceptive Practices

  • Websites created solely for SEO manipulation or link farming
  • Misleading or deceptive business practices
  • Fake reviews, testimonials, or endorsements
  • Mass-generated websites with no genuine business purpose

AI-Generated Content Guidelines

Content generated by our AI system is designed to be helpful and accurate, but users should be aware that:

  • AI-generated text may contain inaccuracies and should be reviewed and verified before publishing
  • Users are responsible for the accuracy of all content on their websites, including AI-generated content
  • AI-generated content should be customized to accurately represent your business
  • You should not rely solely on AI-generated content for legal, medical, financial, or other professional advice

Content Moderation

Megabyte LLC reserves the right to:

  • Review any content hosted on the platform
  • Remove or disable access to content that violates this policy
  • Suspend or terminate accounts that repeatedly violate these guidelines
  • Report illegal content to appropriate law enforcement authorities

Reporting Violations

If you encounter content on Project Sites that you believe violates this Content Policy, please report it to us:

  • Email: abuse@megabyte.space
  • Include the URL of the offending website and a description of the violation

We will investigate all reports and take appropriate action within a reasonable timeframe.

Consequences of Violations

Violations of this Content Policy may result in:

  1. Warning: A notification to the account holder about the violation
  2. Content Removal: Removal of the offending content or website
  3. Account Suspension: Temporary suspension of the account
  4. Account Termination: Permanent termination of the account and all associated websites
  5. Legal Action: Referral to law enforcement for illegal content

The severity of action taken will depend on the nature and severity of the violation, whether it is a first or repeated offense, and applicable legal requirements.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Content Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via email or through a notice on our platform. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
